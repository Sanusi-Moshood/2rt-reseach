Product

2RT is a factory of investment solutions in the digital space.

Meet IOD

IOD (In-Out Digital) is our first basket designed for conservative investors. It uses statistical learning methods to deploy risk only when the market momentum is positive (and cuts exposure otherwise, no shorts allowed). IOD has been selected to be deployed on Coinshares-Napoelon (multi-billions USD crypto asset manager) bots platform.


In the Pipeline

IOD-Y (In-Out Digital + Yield) is the above momentum strategy combined to DeFi and volatility strategies in order for investors to earn yields. Finally, PAC – Pairs Arbitrage for Cryptos– is our long-short solution relying on machine learning. Both baskets will be available soon.
